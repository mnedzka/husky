Strategy

We start off by building solid foundations for future product development. The first step is to track the customer journey and carry out an in-depth investigation of user needs. On the basis of this we can start to create the Business Model Canvas to make sure we have a full understanding of all stakeholders’ needs and define the roadmap towards building a new product or improving what already exists.

Product Audit – review the user experience, metrics review, brand, graphic design, content and competition

User Research – user interviews and testing, stakeholder interviews, customer inquiry, survey analysis

Strategy Discovery – customer journey map, canvas business model, persona creation, brand strategy, product strategy

Design

We build products from scratch, accompanying the client from the very start. We create a team from our group of experts to make sure we have the right skills needed to build a consistent and recognizable product. We believe that even though the design interface is crucial, what really drives a successful product is content – our past experience with data driven products has taught us how to make it tangible.

Brand Design – logos, visual guidelines, product websites

UX Design – user experience flow, product prototyping, interactive mockups

Graphic Design – app visuals, illustration & icons design, interface design

Improvements

Product building is an ongoing project that requires continual improvement and change in order to keep on track . We help you by highlighting the strengths of your business and sharing best practises and experiences and suggesting the most efficient methodologies.

Process Optimization - progress check-in, design iterations, incorporating agile practices

Product Metrics - setting up tools, analyzing metrics, dashboard design

Mentoring & Consultancy - company audits, expert-led workshops, Google Design Sprints
